There is currently no evidence of an increase in miscarriages as a result of 
COVID-19 immunization. This claim was made based on United Kingdom data.
Health officials in the U.K. said the figures are being taken out of context. 
The article doesn’t account for the increase in the vaccination rates over 
time, or the average number of miscarriages that occur naturally.
